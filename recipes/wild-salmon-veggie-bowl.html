Veggie Salmon Bowl

Description

Succulent salmon flaked over a bed of healthy vegetables makes a delicious, protein-packed salad that's also low-calorie, gluten-free and rich in beneficial omega-3 fats

Ingredients

  • 2 Carrots
  • 1 Large Courgette
  • 2 cooked beetroot, diced
  • 1/3 pack of dill, chopped
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 280g poached or canned wild salmon
  • 2 tbsp capers in vinegar

Steps

  1. Shred the carrots and courgette into long spaghetti strips with a julienne peeler or spiralizer, and pile onto two plates.
  2. STEP 2 Stir the beetroot, balsamic vinegar, chopped dill and red onion together in a small bowl, then spoon on top of the veg. Flake over chunks of the salmon and scatter with the capers and extra dill, if you like.
